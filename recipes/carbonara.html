Carbonara

Carbonara is an Italian pasta dish made with eggs, hard cheese, cured pork, and black pepper.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 lb of pancetta or thick cut bacon
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 egg yolk
  • 4 ounces of Parmigiano-Reggiano
  • Ground black pepper
  • Kosher salt
  • 16 ounces of spaghetti noodles
  • 1/4 cup of pasta water

Steps

  1. Dice 1/2 lb of pancetta or thick cut bacon
  2. Combine 3 large eggs and 1 egg yolk
  3. Grate 4 ounces of Parmigiano-Reggiano
  4. Combine grated cheese and eggs together until homogenous
  5. Season cheese and egg mixture with black pepper
  6. Add pacetta or thick cut bacon to cold pan and turn the heat to medium. Once fully cooked but not too crispy, turn off the heat
  7. Add noodles to a large pot of heavily salted water
  8. Add the cooked noodles to the pan with the cooked meat
  9. Add the egg and cheese mixture into the pan and mix rigorously
  10. Plate up and enjoy!
